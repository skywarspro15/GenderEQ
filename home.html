What's the problem?

Click on these cards to learn more.

  • Gender issues in the workplace

    In some workplaces, recruiters judge people based on their gender.

    People can post harmful and offensive posts against a certain gender.

  • Lack of political attention

    Some politicians keep giving deaf ears to these issues.



The solution

Solutions to these problems

  • Stop the stereotyping.

    Stop believing that "men is stronger than women". That's not true.

  • Promote gender equality.

    Instead of posting harmful and offensive posts to a certain gender, promote gender equality instead.

  • Start taking action.

    If we just keep exclaiming about the topic, politicians would keep ignoring it.

About GenderEQ

GenderEQ is a community-driven group project by Tranch Research Institute.

Our goal for this project is to solve today's problems people face.
In each quarter of the year, we make different content on this site. and currently, we try and mitigate today's gender issues.

Through modern ways of developing solutions, we're trying to mitigate today's gender issues.
